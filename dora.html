Doraemon's Friends

Full Name: Nobita Nobi

Age: 10 years old

School: He studies at a local elementary school in Tokyo, Japan

Class: 4th grade (later shown in 5th grade in some episodes)

Personality: Kind-hearted, honest, emotional, lazy in studies, and often unlucky.

Strengths: Very kind, loyal to friends, good at shooting games, strong sense of justice.

Weaknesses: Poor in academics, afraid of exams, physically weak, and easily scared.

About Nobita: Nobita Nobi is a simple and relatable child who represents an ordinary student. Though he struggles in school and often gets into trouble, his good heart and caring nature make him special. He shows that kindness and friendship matter more than being perfect.

Full Name: Shizuka Minamoto

Age: 10 years old

School: She studies at the same local elementary school in Tokyo, Japan.

Class: 4th grade (later shown in 5th grade in some episodes)

Personality: Kind, gentle, polite, intelligent, and caring.

Strengths: Good in studies, emotionally mature, supportive friend, strong moral values.

Weaknesses: Sometimes too trusting and easily worried about others.

About Shizuka: Shizuka Minamoto is a sweet and responsible girl who represents kindness and balance. She often guides Nobita in the right direction and supports her friends with patience and understanding. Her calm nature and good heart make her one of the most loved characters in Doraemon.

Full Name: Takeshi Gouda (also known as Gian)

Age: 10 years old

School: He studies at the same local elementary school in Tokyo, Japan.

Class: 4th grade (later shown in 5th grade in some episodes)

Personality: Aggressive, loud, bossy, but emotional and protective of friends.

Strengths: Physically strong, confident, leadership qualities, loyal to close friends.

Weaknesses: Short-tempered, poor singer (though he thinks otherwise), misuses strength.

About Gian: Gian is the strong and dominating member of the group who often bullies others, especially Nobita. However, deep inside, he has a caring heart and stands up for his friends in difficult times. His character shows that even tough people can have kindness and emotions hidden beneath their rough behavior.

Full Name: Suneo Honekawa

Age: 10 years old

School: He studies at the same local elementary school in Tokyo, Japan.

Class: 4th grade (later shown in 5th grade in some episodes)

Personality: Clever, talkative, boastful, cunning, and sometimes jealous.

Strengths: Intelligent, good communication skills, creative ideas, socially smart

Weaknesses: Cowardly when alone, overconfident, dependent on Gian for protection

About Suneo: Suneo Honekawa is known for showing off his wealth and gadgets, often making others jealous. Though he sometimes bullies Nobita, he is intelligent and resourceful. His character represents how pride and insecurity can exist together, making him a realistic and relatable character.
